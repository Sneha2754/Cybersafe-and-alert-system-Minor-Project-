Cyber Crime Awareness

Learn about different cyber crimes and how to stay safe online.

Phishing

Fake emails or websites are used to steal passwords, bank details and personal information.

Hacking

Unauthorized access to systems or accounts to steal or modify data.

Identity Theft

Stealing personal information like Aadhaar, passwords, or bank details.

Cyber Bullying

Harassing or threatening someone using online platforms.

Online Fraud

Fake websites, scams, or payments used to steal money online.

Malware

Harmful software like viruses and spyware that damages devices.

Ransomware

Locks your files and demands money to unlock them.

Deepfake Scam

AI-generated fake videos or voices used to scam people.

Password Attack

Hackers try different passwords or leaked data to break accounts.

Social Engineering

Manipulating users to reveal sensitive information like OTPs.

Data Breach

Unauthorized access causing leak of sensitive data.

Fake Apps

Fake applications designed to steal user information.

ATM Fraud

Stealing money using ATM skimming or fake cards.

SIM Swap Fraud

Taking control of your SIM to access OTPs and accounts.

Cyber Stalking

Online harassment or tracking of a person repeatedly.

Email Spoofing

Fake emails sent pretending to be trusted sources.

Keylogging

Records keystrokes to steal passwords and data.

Spyware

Hidden software that secretly monitors user activity.
